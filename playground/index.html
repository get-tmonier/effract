effract playground

A tiny shop — logic in Effect, React for render. All state lives in services: the cart (items, quantity, a derived total) is mutated in the product view and read by the header badge with no props between them; the route is a service atom, not useState. Products are fetched (query) with .suspense loading; a lookup can fail in typed ways (.catch) — #3 is sold out, #9 doesn't exist. Components only yield* and render.
